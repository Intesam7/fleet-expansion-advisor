<h1>Fleet Expansion Advisor</h1>
<h3>Built for 1Now Operators · 1Now Labs Concept</h3>
<hr>
<h2>The Problem</h2>
<p>Every independent car rental operator hits the same wall:<br>
<strong>"Should I buy another car? Which one? Can I actually afford it?"</strong></p>
<p>Today, that decision is made on gut feel. An operator checks their<br>
calendar, sees it's busy, and starts browsing CarGurus. There's no<br>
structured framework inside 1Now — or anywhere else — that synthesizes<br>
existing fleet data into a confident expansion decision.</p>
<p>A single bad call — wrong car, wrong timing — can wipe out 6 months<br>
of net profit. A missed expansion means <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>1</mn><mo separator="true">,</mo><mn>500</mn><mtext>–</mtext></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">1,500–</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height:0.8389em;vertical-align:-0.1944em;"></span><span class="mord">1</span><span class="mpunct">,</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right:0.1667em;"></span><span class="mord">500–</span></span></span></span>2,000/month in<br>
bookings that went to a competitor.</p>
<p>The signal already exists inside 1Now: utilization rates, ADR by<br>
vehicle class, booking patterns. Nobody was connecting it to the<br>
capital decision. This tool does.</p>
<hr>
<h2>The Fix</h2>
<p><strong>Fleet Expansion Advisor</strong> answers three questions operators actually ask:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Do I need another car right now?</strong> — based on fleet utilization<br>
and missed booking signals</li>
<li><strong>What type of car should I add?</strong> — based on demand gaps and ADR<br>
performance by vehicle class</li>
<li><strong>Will it actually make money?</strong> — real ROI calculation with loan,<br>
insurance, and maintenance factored in</li>
</ol>
<hr>
<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Fleet Health Dashboard</strong> — Live utilization rates, ADR, monthly<br>
revenue, and status tags per vehicle</li>
<li><strong>Expansion Signal Meter</strong> — Animated gauge scoring expansion<br>
readiness 0–100 with reasoning cards</li>
<li><strong>Vehicle Recommendation Engine</strong> — 3 ranked vehicle type<br>
recommendations with demand match scores and projected revenue</li>
<li><strong>Interactive ROI Calculator</strong> — Sliders for purchase price, down<br>
payment, loan term, interest rate, ADR, and utilization. Updates<br>
net monthly contribution and break-even timeline in real time</li>
<li><strong>AI Expansion Brief</strong> — Claude-powered natural language<br>
recommendation summarizing the operator's specific situation</li>
<li><strong>12-Month Profit Projection</strong> — Bar chart showing cumulative net<br>
contribution month by month</li>
</ul>

<h2>The Idea</h2>
<p>1Now already helps operators run the fleet they have.<br>
Nobody helps them decide when to grow it.</p>
<p>Fleet expansion is the highest-stakes decision a small operator makes<br>
— it's a <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>25</mn><mo separator="true">,</mo><mn>000</mn><mtext>–</mtext></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">25,000–</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height:0.8389em;vertical-align:-0.1944em;"></span><span class="mord">25</span><span class="mpunct">,</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right:0.1667em;"></span><span class="mord">000–</span></span></span></span>40,000 capital commitment made on instinct. This<br>
tool turns that instinct into a data-backed recommendation, using<br>
data the operator already has inside 1Now.</p>
<p>This is a 1Now Labs concept — designed as a native add-on to the<br>
existing 1Now dashboard, available as a $30/month upsell for Plus<br>
and Enterprise operators.</p>
